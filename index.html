About

Dinos 4 Sale serves to find loving homes for dinosaurs of all shapes and sizes. We started as a rescue home for dinosaurs. Dinos are abused and abandoned an incredible amount of the time by their owners. After fighting the red tape of charity, we found we can actually serve more people and dinosaurs as a for profit business.

Originally owners Jack and Dianne rescued their own dinosaurs - a stegosaurus - 7 and a half years ago. They fell in love with Stella. She quickly became, and remains, part of the family.

Today Jack and Dianne spend their days primarily matching potential owners with amazing dinosaurs. However, they continue keeping their hands in the business by overseeing all aspects - from daily dinos care to connecting with customers to transporting their loving beasts around the world.
